Shearing Machines

Precision Cutting for Heavy-Duty Fabrication

Overview

SRTC manufactures high-precision hydraulic shearing machines suitable for cutting a variety of metal sheets with minimal distortion. Our machines offer high productivity, superior cut accuracy, and robust construction for continuous operation.


Specifications

  • Cutting Thickness: 1 mm to 25 mm (MS)
  • Cutting Length: Up to 4000 mm
  • Blade Type: High Carbon / HCHC Blades
  • Back Gauge: Motorized / NC / CNC
  • Operation: Hydraulic Guillotine or Swing Beam
  • Safety: Finger Guard, Foot Switch, Overload Valve

Types Manufactured at SRTC

  • Hydraulic Guillotine Shearing Machine
  • Swing Beam Shearing Machine
  • NC Controlled Shearing Machine
  • CNC Shearing Line (Customizable)
