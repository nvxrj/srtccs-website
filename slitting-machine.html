Slitting Machines

Efficient Coil Processing, Engineered by Experts

Overview

SRTC designs and manufactures precision slitting machines for sheet and coil processing. Our slitting lines offer high-speed, burr-free, and accurate slits for a wide range of materials including steel, aluminum, and copper.


Specifications

  • Material Thickness: 0.3mm to 8mm
  • Coil Width: Up to 2000 mm
  • Line Speed: Up to 150 m/min
  • Slitting Capacity: 2 to 20 strips per pass
  • Recoiler: Hydraulic or motorized options
  • Control System: PLC + HMI with auto length correction

Types Manufactured at SRTC

  • Mini Slitting Lines
  • Heavy Duty Slitting Lines
  • Manual and Semi-Automatic Slitters
  • High-Speed PLC-Based Slitting Systems
