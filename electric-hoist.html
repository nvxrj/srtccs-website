Electric Hoists

Compact Power, Reliable Lifting

Overview

SRTC electric hoists are compact lifting solutions designed for efficient material handling in workshops, warehouses, and manufacturing plants. Engineered for safety and performance, our hoists deliver smooth lifting and long service life.


Specifications

  • Capacity: 0.5 Ton to 10 Tons
  • Lifting Height: Up to 30 meters
  • Hoisting Speed: 3 to 15 m/min
  • Motor Type: Squirrel cage / Brake motor with insulation class F
  • Wire Rope: Usha Martin / Equivalent IS Standard
  • Control: Pendant push button / Wireless remote

Types Manufactured at SRTC

  • Electric Wire Rope Hoists
  • Chain Electric Hoists
  • Low Headroom Hoists
  • Customized Dual-Speed and Flameproof Hoists
