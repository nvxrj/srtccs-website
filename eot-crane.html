EOT Cranes

Engineered for Strength, Built to Perform

Overview

SRTC manufactures robust and high-performance EOT (Electric Overhead Travelling) Cranes tailored for a wide range of industrial applications. Designed for precision and safety, our cranes are known for reliability, long service life, and low maintenance.


Specifications

  • Lifting Capacity: 1 Ton to 100 Tons
  • Span: Up to 30 meters
  • Crane Type: Single Girder / Double Girder / Underslung / Gantry
  • Hoist Type: Electric Wire Rope Hoist / Chain Hoist
  • Control: Pendant / Radio Remote / Cabin
  • Safety: Limit Switches, Overload Protection, Brake Systems

Types Manufactured at SRTC

  • Single Girder EOT Crane
  • Double Girder EOT Crane
  • Underslung Crane
  • Semi-Gantry & Full Gantry Cranes
  • Customized EOT Cranes for Special Applications
